About Me

Daylin Hull is a NYC based makeup artist with 10+ years of experience with a focus on boudoir, editorial, and anything with a vampy twist.

She began her career working with local Burlesque performers which led to assisting on weddings,eventually leading to editorial features. She has worked on editorials for Tartarus Magazine, Opera Magazine, and Vogue Italia to name a few. She loves being able to explore all paths of makeup with a focus on making anyone and everyone who sits in her chair, feel beautiful in their skin.

Presently, Daylin works in the tristate area. She lives in Astoria, NY with her partner and 3 cats. She has been working with Survivors of Domestic Violence and the LGBTQ community, providing education and products to anyone looking to learn and enhance what they're working with!
